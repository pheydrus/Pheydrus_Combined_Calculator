Canonical lanes:
- HJ = Hero_s Journey lane
- BG = Business Growth lane
- AW = Artist_s Way lane

Ticket ladder:
- Low Ticket -> Medium Ticket -> High Ticket

Folder map (canonical):
- Low Ticket/HJ
- Medium Ticket/HJ
- High Ticket/HJ
- Low Ticket/BG
- Medium Ticket/BG
- High Ticket/BG
- Low Ticket/AW
- Medium Ticket/AW
- High Ticket/AW

How team should use this:
1. Start user in the correct lane first (HJ, BG, or AW).
2. Keep upsells inside the same lane before cross-lane bundle offers.
3. Use this sequence for launches and email planning:
   - Low Ticket lane entry -> Medium Ticket lane offer -> High Ticket lane program
4. Only cross-sell another lane after the user has completed Medium or High in current lane.

Lane-specific pathways:
- HJ pathway:
   Low Ticket/HJ -> Medium Ticket/HJ -> High Ticket/HJ
- BG pathway:
   Low Ticket/BG -> Medium Ticket/BG -> High Ticket/BG
- AW pathway:
   Low Ticket/AW -> Medium Ticket/AW -> High Ticket/AW

Planning guidance for marketer:
- For each lane, create 3 campaign blocks:
   1) Entry offer (Low)
   2) Upgrade offer (Medium)
   3) Core transformation offer (High)
- Build email automations per lane, not per random product.
- Keep proof/testimonials matched to lane context (HJ proof for HJ emails, etc).
