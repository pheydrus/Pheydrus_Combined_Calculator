Part 2 — Your Human Design Type

Your HD Type determines what kind of content leaves the strongest impression and how your energy is designed to attract and create. Click yours to explore.

Manifestor
~9% of the population
Strategy: Inform before you act
Manifestors are initiators — you don't wait for permission. Your content carries a natural authority and boldness that people feel. You're here to start movements, not follow them.
Best content style: Bold declarations, manifestos, "I decided" statements. You don't ask — you announce. Your most powerful content will feel slightly provocative.
Examples: Adele, Maya Angelou, George W. Bush, Jack Nicholson
Generator
~37% of the population
Strategy: Wait to respond
Generators have a powerful, sustainable life force. Your content is most magnetic when it comes from genuine excitement — your audience can FEEL when you're lit up vs. going through the motions.
Best content style: Response-based content. React to trends, questions, or DMs. "Someone asked me..." hooks. High-energy, sustained momentum formats.
Examples: Oprah Winfrey, Dalai Lama, Beyoncé, Elvis Presley
Manifesting Generator
~33% of the population
Strategy: Wait to respond, then inform
MGs are multi-passionate, fast-moving, and wired for efficiency. You skip steps that others take. Your content thrives when it shows your range and speed — don't apologize for pivoting.
Best content style: Multi-topic content, fast-paced reels, "plot twist" narratives. Your pivots and pivots-within-pivots are fascinating to watch. Show the journey, not just the destination.
Examples: Angelina Jolie, Freddie Mercury, Marie Forleo, Tony Robbins
Projector
~21% of the population
Strategy: Wait for the invitation
Projectors are guides. You see systems, people, and energy in ways others miss. Your content works best when it positions you as the expert who's been invited to share — not pushing, but pulling.
Best content style: Deep insights, "what I noticed about..." observations, wisdom-forward content. One powerful piece of guidance per video. Authority through precision, not volume.
Examples: Barack Obama, Princess Diana, Rihanna, Nelson Mandela
Reflector
~1% of the population
Strategy: Wait a lunar cycle before major decisions
Reflectors are the rarest type — you're a mirror of the collective. You reflect the health of the communities and spaces you're in. Your content is most powerful when it articulates what everyone else is feeling but can't name.
Best content style: Cultural commentary, trend analysis, "the real reason everyone is feeling X right now" content. You're a barometer of the collective — use that. Don't rush your content decisions.
Examples: Ammachi, Sandra Bullock, Fyodor Dostoevsky

Your voice is

AI doesn't create authority. It reveals it. Your job isn't to sound better — it's to sound truer.

The Core Principle

"Forcing 'a voice' kills sales. You're not meant to communicate like everyone else. Once your voice is clear, your message stops leaking energy."

How most people use AI
How we use AI

The Million-Dollar Storytelling Formula

Divide your video length into 4 equal parts. Each section gets equal time — ~15 seconds each for a 60-second video.

Dream
What does your customer's dream outcome look like? Make them feel seen.
Nightmare
What's their biggest nightmare? The fear keeping them stuck right now?
Obstacle
The real problem they haven't named yet — your unique insight.
New Dream (Your Offer)
The bridge from nightmare to dream — your solution.
