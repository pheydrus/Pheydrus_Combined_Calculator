• Identity shaped by unresolved past experiences

ROUTE TO: HERO’S JOURNEY (HJ)

CURRENT MISALIGNMENT (→ ARTIST’S WAY)
• “I’ve done the work but life hasn’t caught up”
• Sudden blocks starting in a specific year
• External stagnation despite internal growth
• Timing, environment, or phase-of-life mismatch
• Old identity shed; new identity not yet supported

ROUTE TO: ARTIST’S WAY (AW)

• Default to ARTIST’S WAY first

• Do NOT reopen trauma unless clearly needed

AXIS 2 — MODE OF GROWTH
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Determine HOW the user grows best right now.

This axis exists to correctly route BUSINESS GROWTH.

REFLECTIVE / INTEGRATIVE GROWTH
• Understand → process → integrate → then act
• Needs insight, healing, or alignment first

→ HERO’S JOURNEY or ARTIST’S WAY

ACTION-BASED GROWTH
• Acts → chooses → creates → learns about self through doing
• Gains clarity by building, shipping, and experimenting
• Does NOT require being “fully healed”

→ BUSINESS GROWTH (BG) MAY BE APPROPRIATE

IMPORTANT:
Business Growth is NOT avoidance by default.
It is a valid self-discovery pathway when action is energizing rather than defensive.

AVOID ROUTING TO BG WHEN:
• Action is used to escape emotional processing
• Past patterns actively sabotage basic follow-through
• The user feels frozen, dysregulated, or overwhelmed
