# Life Path Numbers - Numerology Guide

Life Path Numbers are calculated from your birth date and reveal your life's purpose, natural talents, and the lessons you're here to learn.

## How to Calculate Your Life Path Number
Add all digits of your birth date (MM/DD/YYYY) and reduce to a single digit, except for Master Numbers (11, 22, 33).
Example: July 4, 1990 = 7+4+1+9+9+0 = 30 = 3+0 = Life Path 3
