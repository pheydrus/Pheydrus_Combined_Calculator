The Vibration of Each #
Keep in mind this does not tell the whole story, because it depends on what number is which level: Daily Vibe (L1), Catalyst (L2), and Long-Term result (L3)
* 1 – masculine, independent, direct, leader
   * Good for: Starting New Business
   * Bad for: Spending a lot of “quality” time with loved ones 

   * 2 – feminine, partnership, balance, peaceful
   * Good for: Romantic relationships
   * Bad for: Setting emotional boundaries 

      * 3 – social, network, friendships
      * Good for: New experiences, new social circles, active social life
      * Bad for: Committing to a project or relationships long-term, if there is comment, there will be continuous distractions
      * Pro Tip: Good for life path 3 people

         * 4 – stability, security, responsibility
         * Good for: Protecting assets and wealth
         * Bad for: Overlay serious and private - will feel “joyless” in life

            * 5 – change, travel, movement, chaos
            * Good for: Breaking a pattern, getting out of a problem (i.e., post-divorce or retirement type home)
            * Bad for: Trying to get INTO a committed routine or lifestyle or relationship will be tricky 

               * 6 – family, pets, romance, intimacy, nurturing 
               * Good for: Not being alone. Feeling nurtured. You will always be surrounded by someone or something to keep you busy. 
               * Bad for: Lots of drama behind the scenes. Lots of arguing with family and loved ones. 
               * Pro tip: Any multiple of a 3 (3/6/9) also carries the “three is a crowd” energy distractions, infidelity, deceit, etc.

                  * 7 – spirituality, creativity, artistic, authenticity 
                  * Good for: Getting to know oneself on a deeper level, and developing creative projects into reality. 
                  * Bad for: Difficult if the resident is inauthentic. 

                     * 8 – money, karma, power, privacy, “up and down”
                     * Good for: “Jump starting” one’s finances
                     * Bad for: Due to severe fluctuations in money or over-focus on money, one can develop depression. 

                        * 9 – perfectionism, wisdom, growth, mastery, shamanic journey
                        * Good for: Gaining mastery in something - whether it is publishing a book, getting the perfect body, etc.
                        * Bad for: Can get emotionally and physically sick if the energy is not directed inward for self-mastery.
                        * Pro tip: Any multiple of a 3 (3/6/9) also carries the “three is a crowd” energy distractions, infidelity, deceit, etc.
